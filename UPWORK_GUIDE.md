# Upwork Profile Optimization Guide

## 1. Title (Headline)

**Bad:** "Software Developer" / "Full Stack Developer"
**Good:** "Data Visualization & Dashboard Developer | React · D3.js · Chart.js | PostgreSQL Expert"

Keep it specific. Combine your niches with pipe separator.

## 5. Hourly Rate Strategy

- **Starting rate:** $25–35/hour
- After 5 completed jobs with 5-star reviews → raise to $45–60/hour
- After 15+ jobs → raise to $65–100/hour

Why start low? Upwork's algorithm favors new freelancers with lower rates + high job success. Build the reviews first.

## 6. Proposal Template

When applying to jobs, use this structure:

```
Hi [CLIENT NAME],

I took a close look at your project — [1 sentence showing you understood their specific need].

I've built similar [dashboards/APIs/data tools] recently. For example, I just launched a real-time crypto dashboard that pulls live data from REST APIs and renders interactive charts — you can see it here: [PORTFOLIO LINK].

For your project, I'd approach it like this:
1. [First step — show thinking]
2. [Second step]
3. [Third step — delivery]

A few questions to understand better:
• [Technical question]
• [Business question]

Happy to hop on a quick call or share more relevant samples. I'm available during your business hours.

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

**Key rules:**
- NEVER use "Dear Sir/Madam" — use their name if available, or just "Hi"
- NEVER say "I can do this for cheap" — focus on value
- ALWAYS include at least one specific question about their project
- ALWAYS reference your portfolio/demo link
- Keep it under 200 words
